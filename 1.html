There are 3 types of options

  1. In the Money (ITM)
  2. Out of the Money (OTM)>
  3. At the Money (ATM)

In the Money

Search on Google for ITM and you will see how volatility and extrinsic values will differ based on how close the strike prices are to the stock price. Option prices are based on the underlying movement of the stock prices This is an important point of trading options.
